How It Works

Step 1: Farmer Lists Crop

Farmers upload crop details, images, and expected price.

Step 2: Buyer Chooses

Buyers browse listings and compare prices easily.

Step 3: Direct Deal

Buyer contacts farmer and completes the transaction.

About Us

AgriLink Pakistan is a student-led initiative from BS Computer Science aimed at solving agricultural market challenges using technology. Our goal is to empower farmers, ensure fair pricing, and promote sustainable agricultural growth in Pakistan.
